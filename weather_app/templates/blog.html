Vignan's Institute Of Information Technology

About Vignan

Vignan's Institute of Information Technology (VIIT) was established in 2002 in Visakhapatnam, the City of Destiny, to provide quality education and empower future professionals. The institute attained Autonomous status in 2017 and is accredited with NAAC (A+) and NBA, showcasing its commitment to academic excellence.

About CSE

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at Vignan's Institute of Information Technology is dedicated to providing students with the knowledge and skills required to excel in the tech industry. With state-of-the-art laboratories, experienced faculty, and industry collaborations, CSE at VIIT ensures students are well-prepared for the future.

  • Modern AI & Machine Learning Labs
  • Industry-driven curriculum
  • Excellent placement opportunities
  • Collaborations with top tech companies
